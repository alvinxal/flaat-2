Projects

What kind of projects does Modio Studio work on?

We specialize in branding, web design, product design, and creative campaigns. Each project is tailored to the client’s needs.

Do you work with startups as well as established companies?

Yes. We love shaping identities for early-stage startups and also partner with established businesses looking to grow or rebrand.

How long does a typical project take?

Most projects are completed within 4–8 weeks, depending on complexity. Larger or multi-service projects may take longer, with timelines clearly outlined in advance.

Get Started

How does payment work?

We typically require a 50% deposit to begin and the remaining balance upon project completion. Payment plans may be available depending on scope.

What do I need to provide before we start?

A project brief, any existing brand assets, and your goals or vision. If you’re unsure, we’ll guide you through what’s needed.

How can I start a project with you?

Simply reach out through our contact page. Share your project details, and we’ll follow up to discuss next steps and schedule a call.
